8 September 23 - Conference for Hands on Testing

Hands on Testing 2023 – Smart Testing welcomes Gonzalo Diamend who will join us with his talk “The Power of Synergy: How Manual and Automation QA complement each other” He will share with us his experience in the field of QA and how the combination of these two approaches can increase efficiency and productivity in software projects. Join us to discover the synergy that exists between manual and automatic testing and how you can incorporate them into your projects. Do not miss it!
